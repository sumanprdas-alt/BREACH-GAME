# PROJECT BREACH — Story Bible (One-Pager)

---

## THE WORLD

**Year: 2089.** After decades of fragile peace, a shadowy paramilitary syndicate known as **CRIMSON DAWN** has seized control of a remote archipelago in the South Pacific — **Archipelago Sigma**. Hidden beneath its volcanic islands lies a network of underground facilities housing **Project Starkfire**: a stolen superweapon capable of triggering a global extinction-level event. The weapon harnesses an unstable energy source — a concentrated plasma core derived from deep-earth minerals — that, once activated, would send a cascading pulse across every major city on Earth. No missiles. No warheads. Just a single pulse, and civilization goes dark.

World governments are paralyzed. Diplomacy has failed. Satellite imagery shows the weapon is 72 hours from activation.

---

## THE TEAM

Three operatives. No backup. No extraction plan.

**KANE** — The Commander. Former black-ops unit leader, dishonorably discharged for disobeying orders to save his team. Cold, precise, speaks only in objectives. He doesn't fight for countries — he fights for the people standing next to him. *"We go in together. We come out together. Or we don't come out at all."*

**VIPER** — The Ghost. Ex-intelligence asset turned freelance infiltrator. No government claims her. No record exists. She moves through kill zones like smoke — by the time you see her, it's already over. Recruited by Kane after she single-handedly dismantled a Crimson Dawn cell in Jakarta. *"I don't need a plan. I need a door."*

**IRONSIDE** — The Wall. Combat engineer and heavy weapons specialist. Built like the machines he operates. Lost his brother to a Crimson Dawn attack three years ago. Every enemy is personal. Carries enough firepower to level a compound, and the scars to prove he's been inside more than one. *"Point me at the biggest thing on the island. I'll make it smaller."*

---

## THE MISSION — 5 STAGES

**STAGE 1: JUNGLE BREACH**
*"Get boots on the ground."*
Beach insertion under fire. Dense jungle, patrol squads, watchtower turrets. The team fights uphill toward the island's outer perimeter wall. Boss: **The Gate** — a massive fortified wall bristling with auto-cannons. Break through or die trying.

**STAGE 2: THE CORRIDOR**
*"We're inside. Stay sharp."*
Indoor base infiltration. Tight corridors, security systems, laser grids, armored soldiers. A claustrophobic shift from open jungle to steel-and-concrete tunnels. Boss: **Sentinel** — a walking mech that patrols the inner sanctum.

**STAGE 3: THE CASCADE**
*"The island is alive."*
Vertical ascent up a waterfall cliff face and through natural cave systems. The terrain itself is the enemy — collapsing platforms, rushing water, enemies attacking from above. Boss: **Hydra Battery** — a multi-cannon artillery nest carved into the cliff.

**STAGE 4: THE FURNACE**
*"This is where they build it."*
Deep inside the weapons facility. Industrial hellscape — molten conduits, rotating machinery, conveyor systems carrying weapon components. The air burns. Boss: **The Forge Master** — a shielded command platform controlling the assembly line.

**STAGE 5: THE CORE**
*"Shut it down. Whatever it takes."*
The final descent into the Starkfire chamber. Alien-organic architecture — the deeper you go, the less human everything looks. The plasma core has begun to mutate the facility itself. Walls pulse. The floor breathes. Boss: **Starkfire** — the weapon itself has become sentient. A towering biomechanical horror that must be destroyed before the countdown reaches zero.

---

## THE STAKES

If Starkfire fires, every power grid, every hospital, every defense system on Earth goes silent. Permanently. Eight billion people plunged into darkness. Crimson Dawn rises from the ashes as the only force left standing.

Three soldiers. Five stages. One shot.

---

## GAME NAME: **BREACH**

**Why BREACH:**
- **Short** — one word, one syllable, instant impact
- **Action-first** — it's a verb, it's what you DO in the game (breach walls, breach defenses, breach the core)
- **Brandable** — easy to search, easy to say in any language, zero confusion
- **Thematic** — the entire game is one long breach operation, from jungle wall to final core
- **Scalable** — works for sequels: *Breach: Starkfire*, *Breach 2: Dark Horizon*, *Breach Online*
- **Visual** — the word itself looks good on a title screen, on merch, on a logo

**Alternate title options if BREACH is taken:**
1. **BREACH POINT** — adds specificity, still clean
2. **DEADBREACH** — compound word, more aggressive
3. **BREACH ZERO** — implies the countdown, the finality
